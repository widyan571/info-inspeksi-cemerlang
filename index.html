Welcome to Our Website

PT Indo Kaya Energi (IKE) was established in Indonesia in 2005 to provide products and services to the Indonesia Energy Industry. IKE is a wholly owned Indonesia company, focusing on providing first class offering in specialised range of products and services. Our focus is on developing our Indonesia business and assets through internal investment and technology partnerships with renowned international companies. A prime example is our partnership with Oil States Industries who have licensed IKE for the manufacture/sale of conductor and premium connections since 2005. IKE has also invested in technology and our personnel to have a specialised product line of Tubular Running Services, as well as Conductor Hammering and Casing Running Equipment.
